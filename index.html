02Research

What I’m Working On

i.

Science of science & funding policy

How research funding allocations shape what gets discovered, the direction of inquiry, and the structure of scientific careers.

ii.

Research data policy

Open science, data sharing incentives, and how disclosure mandates affect research productivity. Recent work in Research Policy.

iii.

Patent policy & antitrust

Patent consolidation, university technology transfer, and the role of antitrust enforcement in spurring follow-on innovation.

iv.

Innovation & competition

Strategic behavior of firms in technology markets — from patent assertion to defensive aggregation.

v.

Emerging technology

Identifying and tracing technological emergence in fields like nanomedicine, synthetic biology, and quantum computing.

vi.

ML / AI & innovation

How exclusive data access shapes corporate research on machine learning, and implications for competition policy.

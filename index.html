02Research

What I’m working on

i.

Science of science

How scientific knowledge is produced, evaluated, and diffused — treating science itself as the object of empirical study.

ii.

Economics of science

Incentive structures, careers, and resource allocation that shape research productivity and direction.

Patent policy
